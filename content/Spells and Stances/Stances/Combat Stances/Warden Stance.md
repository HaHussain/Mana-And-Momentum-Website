# Stance
The Warden's Stance and Techniques are about reducing the damage you and your allies take. 

|               | Warden Stance                                                                                                                         |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Passive:**  | Your [[Rules/Actions/Tags/Reaction\|Reaction]] [[Strike\|Strikes]] have [[Advantage]] to [[Hit]] and apply [[Restricted]] on [[Hit]]. |
| **Momentum:** | Each time you reduce the damage your allies would have taken in a turn, you gain 1 [[Momentum]].                                      |
| **Tags:**     | [[Spells and Stances/Stances/index\|Stance]], [[Locked]]: [[Knight]]                                                                  |
