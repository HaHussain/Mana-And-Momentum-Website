# Stances
Stances are specific methods of combating a problem.
They have a passive ability, which while the character is in that Stance, they gain the benefit from.
They have a [[Momentum]] Trigger, which allows the character to gain Momentum while in that Stance.
They have several [[#Techniques]] that the character can spend [[Momentum]] on to use while in that Stance.

If a character is in Stance and [[Switch Stance|Switch Stances]], they lose all [[Momentum]].

# Techniques
Techniques are specific actions that can be taken by expending [[Momentum]] while in the necessary [[#Stances|Stance]].
[[Momentum]] is only spent when that action is taken.
