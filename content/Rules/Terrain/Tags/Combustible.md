> [[Obscured]], [[Hazardous]]: # [[Elemental Type#Fire|Fire]]
> Creatures starting their turn or entering it on their turn take # [[Elemental Type#Fire|Fire]].
> It and 1 square above become [[Obscured]] if made [[Wet]] or targeted by a [[Elemental Type#Water|Water]] ability.
