When a [[Elemental Type#Lightning|Lightning]] ability targets wet terrain or a character in wet terrain, all targets in same contiguous wet area take 1 dice roll of damage.
When a [[Elemental Type#Ice|Ice]] ability targets wet terrain it instead becomes [[Slippery]].
When a [[Elemental Type#Fire|Fire]] ability targets wet terrain it instead becomes [[Obscured]] until the end of the next round.
