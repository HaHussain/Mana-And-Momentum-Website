A [[PC]] can take a Shallow Rest in either a safe and comfortable environment like a city, in which case it will take 2 hours, or when safe in a dangerous and uncomfortable environment, in which case the [[PC]] spend 8 hours to do so and must remain safe for that time to gain the benefit.

When a character takes a shallow rest, they may do the following things:
- Regain ([[Health Points|Max HP]]/10 (Round Down) + [[Fortitude|FOR]]) [[Health Points|HP]].
- Take the [[Reclaim]] action to regain 1 [[Spells and Stances/Spells/index|Mana]] committed for a day.
- Reduce their [[Exert#Exertion|Exertion]] by 1.
