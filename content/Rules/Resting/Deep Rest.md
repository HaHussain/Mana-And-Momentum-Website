A [[PC]] can only take a Deep Rest in a safe and comfortable environment, such as an Inn or their own home where it takes 8 hours.
A [[PC]] can only take 1 Deep Rest every 24 hours.

When a character takes a Deep Rest, they may do the following things:
- Regain ([[Health Points|Max HP]]/2 + [[Fortitude|FOR]]) [[Health Points|HP]]
- Take the [[Reclaim]] action to regain all [[Spells and Stances/Spells/index|Mana]]
- Reduce their [[Exert#Exertion|Exertion]] by ([[Exert#Exertion|Max Exertion]]/2)
