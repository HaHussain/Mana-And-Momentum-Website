An organisation represents a collection of people and not necessarily an organised one.
There are multiple types of organisations that represent how they act, an organisation can be more than one type if they are influential in more than one sphere.

Some example types include: Criminal, Economic, Folk, Governmental, Information, Magical, Military.

Each organisation has the following traits and represents their capabilities. Each trait can have a value from 0 to 5.

Size: This represents the number of people within the organisation.
Effectiveness: The skill level of the organisation from novice to elite members.

Might: Physical power. Magical and Military Organisations often have high values.
Influence: Social power. Information and some Criminal organisations often have high values.
Assets: Economic power. Economic Organisations have high values.
Secrets: Informational power. Information and Magical organisations can often have a lot of these.

An organisation grows by increasing these values and an organisation can be destroyed by reducing 2 or more of these traits to 0 and removing any Leaders.
