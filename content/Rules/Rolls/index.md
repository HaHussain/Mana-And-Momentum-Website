This game uses rolling dice as the method of determining what happens in a world full of uncertainty with too many factors to consider.
By default a roll is made by rolling a d20 and adding a modifier.

When rolling, a player may only add 1 additional die to the d20 from their own abilities, and 1 additional die from each other player from their abilities.
In general a player can only add 1 additional die from the GM due to circumstances.

# Roll types
Rolls are generally one of these 5 types:

Crafting, Combat, Exploration, Social, and Luck.

# Roll Parts
A roll consists of two parts that are added together, the Dice Value and the Flat Value.

The Dice Value is the sum of all numbers rolled on the dice. (Also handling any taking highest / lowest, explosions, etc.)

The Flat Value is the static number added to the Dice Value.

## Nat
The Natural Value of a d20 roll is the Dice Value of just the d20 (ignoring any other dice that may be rolled alongside).

On a Nat 20 on a [[Hit]] roll, a [[Strike]] is a [[Critical Strike]].
