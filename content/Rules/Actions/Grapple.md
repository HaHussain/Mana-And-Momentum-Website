A grapple is a special type of [[Strike]] that grabs a [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]] in a way to stop them from moving.
This might be a full body lock in a fight, on a stern hand on the shoulder, or a flirtatious chin lift.
Maintaining a grapple occupies a free hand. 

|                     | Grapple                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Tempo:**          | [[Strike]]                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| **Target:**         | 1 Adjacent [[Rules/Characters/index\|Character]]                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Duration:**       | Indefinite                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| **Pre-Requisites:** | A free hand                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| **Action:**         | Roll a ??? (With [[Size]] modifier)<br>On a Success, you apply [[Grappled]] to the [[Rules/Characters/index\|Character]].<br>While Grappling, you can move the [[Rules/Characters/index\|Character]] with you whenever you move. |
