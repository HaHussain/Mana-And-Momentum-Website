The Free [[Tempo]] tag means you can take this action as an additional action in addition to the usual 2, ignoring the [[Rules/Actions/index#Unique Action Restriction|Unique Action Restriction]] but you do not receive your normal move on a Free turn.

You take the Free action at the tempo listed or the usual tempo for that action if an action is listed.

There is no limit to the number of Free actions you can take in a round.
