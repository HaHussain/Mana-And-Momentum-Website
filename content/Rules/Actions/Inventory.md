When you take an Inventory action, you can do one of the following:
- Load a [[Reload]] Weapon
- Stow a [[Items/index|Items]] in your Hands and Draw an [[Items/index|Item]] to replace it
- Draw a [[Consumables|Consumable]] and immediately consume it, if it is a [[Strike]], this action also counts as an [[Attack]] action for the [[Rules/Actions/index#Unique Action Restriction|Unique Action Restriction]].
