A Strike is an attack with a [[Weapons|Weapon]] you are holding.

It consists of a [[Hit]] roll, where if the roll exceeds the target's AC, it hits and you roll damage.

Then a [[Damage]] roll, which depends on the [[Weapons|Weapon]] and any abilities you may have which is then subtracted from the targets [[Health Points|HP]].
