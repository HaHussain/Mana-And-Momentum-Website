When a character takes a [[Move]] action they can Jump instead of Moving.

The character picks a point they can reach with a [[Arcing|Arc]].

A character can jump a distance of [[Strength|STR]] + 2 squares (minimum 1/2).
A character can jump a height of [[Strength|STR]] / 2 squares.
