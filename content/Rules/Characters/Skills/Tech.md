Technology represents an individual's capabilities on technology, industry, and engineering.

# [[Dexterity]]

[[Dexterity]] Tech represents careful fiddling with technology, soldering, engineering with small minute parts.


# [[Intelligence]]

[[Intelligence]] Tech represents pure engineering, designing and creation of purely technical constructs as well as of knowing the new spirits of technology.


# [[Instinct]]

[[Instinct]] Tech is used to manipulate and control technology constructs, often vehicle handling.
