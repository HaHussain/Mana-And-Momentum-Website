Traverse is a skill representing the skills to move, climb and swim.

# [[Strength]]

[[Strength]] Traverse represents the ability use physical strength to move, to pull yourself over ledges and swim through rapids.


# [[Dexterity]]

[[Dexterity]] Traverse represents the ability to use finesse to move, knowing where to place your feet in climbing to kick off and get better angles.
