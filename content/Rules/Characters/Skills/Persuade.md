Persuasion represents convincing someone using positive emotions.

# [[Intelligence]]

[[Intelligence]] Persuasion represents using logic, understanding and emotions of self-fulfilment, greed, and greater desires to convince someone that agreeing with you aligns with their values or ideals.


# [[Instinct]]

[[Instinct]] Persuasion represents working with baser emotions, such as safety or happiness, to convince someone that agreeing with you aligns with their values or ideals.
