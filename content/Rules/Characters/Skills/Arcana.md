Arcana is the study of magic from the perspective of the mathematics and occult, and the skill of Arcana represents how well versed a character is in handling, scripting and understanding it.

# [[Dexterity]]

The [[Dexterity]] Arcana skill can be used handle the etching of runes, making of effigies, other craft relating to the arcane.

# [[Awareness]]

The [[Awareness]] Arcana skill can be used to sense aura of magic around you, whether it suffuses the atmosphere and terrain or are spell secreted away somewhere nearby.

# [[Intelligence]]

The [[Intelligence]] Arcana skill represents your recall of arcane knowledge and your understanding of it.

# [[Instinct]]

The [[Instinct]] Arcana skill is the ability to communicate and understand Arcane spirits, those that represent abstract concepts or those to do with the soul.
