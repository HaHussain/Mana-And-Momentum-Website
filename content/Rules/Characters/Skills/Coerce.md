Coerce is the skill to use threats and negative emotions to control others to do what you want.


# [[Strength]]

The [[Strength]] Coerce skill allows individuals to threaten someone that you would enact violence to them or someone they care about with your physical might. Knowing and seeing your strength makes them believe it is possible and plausible.


# [[Dexterity]]

The [[Dexterity]] Coerce skill allows individuals to threaten someone that you would enact violence to them or someone they care about with finesse. Knowing and seeing your agility makes them believe it is possible and plausible.


# [[Fortitude]]

The [[Fortitude]] Coerce skill allows a character to strike fear in their enemies by simply showcasing that they are unkillable.


# [[Intelligence]]

The [[Intelligence]] Coerce skill represents the ability to blackmail, rile up distrust, and create fear through logic and explanation, to make the target understand that these are the steps are logically going to occur next.


# [[Instinct]]

The [[Instinct]] Coerce skills represents the ability to imbue fear, terror and disgust in a character by bringing up the fears and hates of that character and then manipulating them whilst their mind is clouded with those emotions.
