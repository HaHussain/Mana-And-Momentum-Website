Physique represents physical exertion and the body.

# [[Strength]]

[[Strength]] Physique represents pure strength, anything anyone would do at the gym, exerting physical force to move heavy objects.


# [[Fortitude]]

[[Fortitude]] Physique represents exerting over a long period of time, where it isn't the act of whether it is possible that is in question, but for long.


# [[Intelligence]]

[[Intelligence]] Physique represents an understanding of the body, medical knowledge and application of it. It can also represent the knowledge of more occult spirits pertaining to the body.


# [[Instinct]]

[[Instinct]] Physique represents a capability of applying medical practises that have been learnt through trial and error. As well as an instinctual understanding of your own body.
