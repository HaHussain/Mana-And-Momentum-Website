There are 12 skills in the game that represent different actions commonly taken by characters that may have a chance of failure.

Each skill can have 0 to 5 [[Levelling Up#Skill Points|Skill Points]] applied, which let you add 0 to 5x your [[Level Bonus]].
Each skill can be used with each stat to perform different actions giving you a total bonus to the roll of:

Bonus = + Stat + [[Level Bonus]]

Listed for each skill is some standard combinations that represent common actions for reference, but do not forget that unlisted combinations may be suitable for uncommon situations.

The skills are:
[[Arcana]],
[[Coerce]],
[[Deceive]],
[[Infiltrate]],
[[Insight]],
[[Nature]],
[[Persuade]],
[[Physique]],
[[Search]],
[[Society]],
[[Tech]],
[[Traverse]]
