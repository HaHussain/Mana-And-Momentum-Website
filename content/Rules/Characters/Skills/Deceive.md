The Deceive skill represents a character's ability to lie, manipulate through implications and half truths, as well as omit the truth. 


# [[Fortitude]]

The [[Fortitude]] Deceive skill deceives others but refusing to give anything away, remaining stoic and silent.


# [[Intelligence]]

The [[Intelligence]] Deceive skill uses half-truths and implication to make it seem like it would be impossible for you to be lying to them, the implied existence of proof that does not exist or doesn't apply.


# [[Instinct]]

The [[Instinct]] Deceive skill uses a calm nature, careful control over yourself, tone, and emotion to make it seem like your deceptions are the same as your truths, and are impossible to differentiate.
