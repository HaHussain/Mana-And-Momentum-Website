The Insight skill represents the ability to analyse people, to find their motives, to read a room, and pierce through deceptions.


# [[Awareness]]

Insight Awareness is a roll to analyse a character's body language, and try to learn their emotional state from hints and clues.


# [[Intelligence]]

Insight Intelligence is about deduction of a character's statement, analysing what they are saying with other information and whether things add up to logically or does their story have holes in it.


# [[Instinct]]

Insight Instinct is about using experience talking to people and the instinct for fear and deception to guide you into a reading the room or particular person, and how they speak in order to see if something feels off about what they are saying.
