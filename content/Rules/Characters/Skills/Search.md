The Search skill is your capability of gaining information by spotting hidden things and deducing more from those things.

# [[Dexterity]]

[[Dexterity]] Search allows a character to frisk and search for things using their hands, as such it can also be used to pick pockets.


# [[Awareness]]

[[Awareness]] Search is the ability to look for things in an area, spotting hidden things and small careful details.


# [[Intelligence]]

[[Intelligence]] Search is the ability to deduce more information, and investigate a scene.


# [[Instinct]]

[[Instinct]] Search is the ability to look through a room, a crowd or a location and get a instinctual sense of how they are feeling and the atmosphere of the location.
