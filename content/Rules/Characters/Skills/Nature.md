# [[Fortitude]]

[[Fortitude]] Nature represents one's ability to survive in nature, amongst the wilderness, hunting and foraging without society and enduring the force of nature.


# [[Awareness]]

[[Awareness]] Nature is a roll that is showcases a character's ability to track and hunt through the wilderness, looking for specific clues and identifying where that may lead them.


# [[Intelligence]]

[[Intelligence]] Nature is about the understanding of nature, knowing which plants are poisonous, understanding how rivers flow and quicksand floods. As well as the understanding of nature spirits, how they tend to act and how they are categorised.


# [[Instinct]]

[[Instinct]] Nature as a roll is about being one with nature, knowing how to handle animals and spirits of nature.
