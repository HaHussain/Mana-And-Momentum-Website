A Reflex Save is an attempt to dodge and move precisely, clearing off acids and ailments.

Your Reflex is the highest of your [[Dexterity|DEX]] and [[Awareness|AWR]] plus half the other.
RFX = Highest of ([[Dexterity|DEX]] + [[Awareness|AWR]]) + 1/2 (Lowest of ([[Dexterity|DEX]] + [[Awareness|AWR]]))
