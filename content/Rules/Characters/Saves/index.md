Saves are used to determine reactive situations where something happens to a [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]].
At the end of each turn a [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]] takes, they can choose one of their saves to reroll, on a success, clearing all [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Conditions]] related to that save.
