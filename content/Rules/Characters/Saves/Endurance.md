An Endurance Save is an attempt to shake off negative effects by using your physical might; pushing past poisons and ailments

Your Endurance is the highest of your [[Strength|STR]] and [[Fortitude|FOR]] plus half the other + [[Level Bonus]].
END = Highest of ([[Strength|STR]] + [[Fortitude|FOR]]) + 1/2 (Lowest of ([[Strength|STR]] + [[Fortitude|FOR]])) + [[Level Bonus]]
