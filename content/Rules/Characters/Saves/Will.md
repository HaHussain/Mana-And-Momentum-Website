A Will Save is an attempt to shake off negative effects by using your mental capabilities; breaking through charms through will and conniving.

Your Will is the highest of your [[Intelligence|INT]] and [[Instinct|INS]] plus half the other.
WILL = Highest of ([[Intelligence|INT]] + [[Instinct|INS]]) + 1/2 (Lowest of ([[Intelligence|INT]] + [[Instinct|INS]]))
