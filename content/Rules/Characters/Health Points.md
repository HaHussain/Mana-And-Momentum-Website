Health Points (HP) represent your physical health and willpower to fight on.

Every player character starts with: 5 Max HP.
And adds [[Fortitude]] HP every 5 [[Levelling Up|levels]] starting at Level 2.

Most [[Classes/index|Classes]] provide 1 HP per Level but some give 2 for tankier [[Classes/index|Classes]] or 1 every 2 levels for squishier [[Classes/index|Classes]].

HP is regained by [[Rules/Resting/index|Resting]].
# Taking Damage
When you take damage, reduce your Health Points by that number. 

If a player reaches 0 HP, they lose all [[Momentum]] and gain +1 [[Exert#Exertion|Exertion]].
If a player is not at Max Exertion, they gain the [[Dying]] Condition. 
If a player is now at Max [[Exert#Exertion|Exertion]] they instead [[Die]].
