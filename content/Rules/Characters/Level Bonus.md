A [[Rules/Characters/index|Characters]] Level Bonus is based on their [[Level]] and is used as a bonus in various rolls.
- It starts at +2
- At Level 12 it becomes +3
- At Level 21 it becomes +4

Many things that allow you to add your Level Bonus, allow you to add them multiple times.
- [[Rules/Characters/Skills/index|Skills]] go from 0 to 5x each depending on [[Levelling Up#Skill Points|Skill Points]]. With the maximum number starting at 2x and increasing as you [[Levelling Up|Level Up]].
- [[Hit]] rolls and [[Defence]] start at 1x and increase automatically as you [[Levelling Up|Level Up]].
- [[Willpower]] only uses 1x.
