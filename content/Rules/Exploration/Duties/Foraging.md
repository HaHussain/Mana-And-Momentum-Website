Foraging is an [[Instinct]] : [[Nature]] roll, for every 5 above 10, you gain 1 Foraging point that must be used within that travel time period. 
Foraging points can be spent to:
- 1 point to feed a [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]]. (Repeatable)
- 1 point to allow another character with another exploration role to reroll their exploration roll. (Repeatable)
- 1 point to restore 1 [[Item Points|Item Point]] for a character or the Fortress. 
- 1 point to uncover a rare resource of the area.
If a character is not fed that day then they must either take a point of [[Exert#Exertion|Exertion]] or take 10 [[Health Points|HP]] damage.
%%Foraging showcases that people cannot exist out in the wilderness forever, that it takes a toll on people.

For a GM - Foraging allows you to reduce the player's resources over time, forcing them to only travel short distances, rest when they get to a scene, and if they're forced to travel long distance, it threatens them by eating up their resources.

For a Player - Foraging fuels the other characters on the journey, by gathering more, you are able to save and enhance other characters, their successes become yours. By succeeding with Foraging, you can journey further, in more treacherous terrain, have more resources later, and give other players more chances to succeed. Likewise by failing with Foraging, your parties resources begin to drain forcing them to retreat back sooner.%%
