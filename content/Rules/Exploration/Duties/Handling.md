Handling is an [[Instinct]] : [[Arcana]] / [[Nature]] / [[Society]] / [[Tech]] roll (depending on steeds), for every 5 above 10, you gain 1 Handling points that must be used within that travel time period. 
Handling points can be spent to:
- Add 1m of Speed to the [[Fortress]] per point.
- Reduce the [[Rules/Exploration/index#Danger Clock|Danger Clock]] by 1 per point.

For a GM - Handling lets you throw combat encounters towards the players at their own pace, keeping the pressure on them but letting them handle it. If they focus on moving fast they may get attacked by monsters and be cockier, if they focus on moving stealthily, they rely on their forager more as their resources are drained.
