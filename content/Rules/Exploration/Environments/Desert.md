# Encounters

## Marauders

Marauders lead by a fearsome Captain aboard a sand sailing ship driven by sand sharks sails by.
- They are starving, Why? Start with half [[Health Points|HP]] and [[Weak]].
- They have been hired by an enemy of yours specifically, what secrets have their overheard?
- They are in the midst of attacking another group. Who? Who is right?

## Sandworm

A sandworm begins to create tremors nearby.
- It is so large the players have no chance of fighting it and must run! How can they escape?
- A vehicle is trapped in the beasts maw, or on its back and something is aboard. What / Who?
- The beast is scarred with a multitude of wounds and weapons including chained harpoons. Who did this? Do any of them seem familiar? Starts with some damage.

## Ruins

Ruins peeking out the sand with a trap hidden and armed
- Who is the ruin by? 
- how large is it? 
- several or just one? 
- Is the trap magical?
- Is there hidden treasure?

Emberleaf Flower Field

## Warren

A warren / cave belonging to something.
- Is it hostile or friendly?
- Is it big or small?
- One burrow or several, can it burrow between them?
- What causes it to leave its burrow?

## Glass Trees

Glass trees that create an enormous canopy that focuses sunlight
- Describe the beauty
- What does the focused sunlight do?
- What does the shade do?

## Glass Sand

Glass sand of elemental colours that charges and release elemental power
- What colours / elements are present? 1? Several?
- What charges them?
- What release them?

## Iron Spires

Iron sand spires with spikes
- How big?
- How breakable?
- Are they still fine sand or have they become chunks?
- Is there anything at the top?
