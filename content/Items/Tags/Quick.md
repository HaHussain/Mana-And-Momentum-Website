This weapon's [[Attack#Weapon Speed|Weapon Speed]] is Quick.

This weapon can make [[Strike|Strikes]] in Quick [[Attack]] instead of Standard [[Attack]].

If the wielder does not make a Quick [[Attack]] this round, they may use this weapon with Standard [[Attack#Weapon Speed|Weapon Speed]].
If the wielder does not make a Quick [[Attack]] or Standard [[Attack]] this round, they may use this weapon with [[Slow]] [[Attack#Weapon Speed|Weapon Speed]].
