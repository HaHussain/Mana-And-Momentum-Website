A Melee weapon is used in close combat and has some amount of [[Range]] from you that it can hit targets from.

Holding a Melee weapon gives you the following [[Rules/Actions/Tags/Reaction|Reaction]]
## Attack of Opportunity

|              | Attack of Opportunity                                                                                                |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Tempo:**   | 3                                                                                                                    |
| **Trigger:** | A [[Rules/Characters/index\|Character]] in your Melee Range moves further away from you (based on your Melee Range). |
| **Action:**  | Make a [[Strike]] against that character before they move.                                                           |
| Tags:        | [[Rules/Actions/Tags/Reaction\|Reaction]] [[Strike]]                                                                 |
