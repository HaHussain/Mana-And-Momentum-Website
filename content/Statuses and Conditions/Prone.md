A Prone character has their [[Speed]] halved and they cannot [[Jump]].

A prone character has [[Disadvantage]] to [[Hit]] with [[Melee]] [[Weapons]].
Characters have [[Advantage]] to [[Hit]] a prone character with [[Melee]] [[Weapons]] and [[Disadvantage]] with [[Ranged]] [[Weapons]].

A character can lose Prone by expending their [[Normal Move]].
