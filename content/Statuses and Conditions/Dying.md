A [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]] at 0 [[Health Points|HP]] is dying.

While Dying, a [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]] can only take 1 [[Rules/Actions/index|Action]] per round, does not get their [[Normal Move]], and cannot make [[Strike|Strikes]].
Each round (10 seconds) at Tempo 0, a Dying [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]] gains 1 [[Exert#Exertion|Exertion]].
Each time a Dying [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]] takes damage, they gain 1 [[Exert#Exertion|Exertion]].

If they reach [[Exert#Exertion|Max Exertion]] they die.
