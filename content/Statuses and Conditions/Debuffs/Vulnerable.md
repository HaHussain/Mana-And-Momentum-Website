When a [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]] with this debuff is targeted by the one of the following, the performer of that action can consume the debuff to add an additional benefit:
- [[Scan|Scanned]]: Learn what [[Rules/Actions/index|Actions]] and all details of those actions this character is going to perform this round.
- [[Strike]]: Double the damage this character takes.
- [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Debuffed]]: Apply 2 more debuffs from the same list (or the [[GM]] decides if the list is exhausted).

[[Mood]]: Vulnerable, Sensitive, Open
