# Conditions

When a Condition is applied it can have one of many durations, 
- If the condition is applied by an ability as an action in [[Combat]] (i.e. over 1 second), it will by default have a duration of [[Combat]] (i.e. a duration of about 1 minute). And thus should be ignored outside of [[Combat]].
- If the condition is applied by a [[Social]] ability outside of [[Combat]] (i.e. over 1 minute, 10 minutes, an hour), it will by default have a duration of [[Scenes|Scene]].
- Other ways to apply a condition will list a duration, including up to the next [[Deep Rest]], or longer.
