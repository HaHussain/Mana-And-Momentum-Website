# Paradigms
Paradigms are specific methods of combating a problem.
They have a passive ability, which while the character is in that Paradigm, they gain the benefit from.
They have a [[Momentum]] Trigger, which allows the character to gain Momentum while in that Paradigm.
They have several [[#Techniques]] that the character can spend [[Momentum]] on to use while in that Paradigm.
