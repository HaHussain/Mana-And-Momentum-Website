# Overview
Surgeon is a class that heals their allies and can debuff their enemies. Clinical and careful.

**Mechanics:** [[Attack]], [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Buff]], [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Debuff]], [[Rest & Recover]]
**Combat Strategy:** Shield and [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Buff]] and remove [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Debuffs]] from allies. Or [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Debuff]] enemies in allies.

# Abilities

## Physiology

The first [[Strike]] in a Round you make against an [[Ally]] with a [[Dexterity|DEX]] [[Weapons|Weapon]], becomes [[Shielding]] instead of [[Damage]].
The first [[Strike]] in a Round you make against an [[Enemy]] with a [[Dexterity|DEX]] [[Weapons|Weapon]], counts as a [[Melee#Called Shot|Called Shot]] with the [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Debuff]] lasting for only this round.

## Something

An [[Ally]] hit by [[#Physiology]] can take the [[Extra]]: [[Rest & Recover#Recover|Recover]] action this round.

When you [[Strike]] and [[Ally]] with [[#Physiology]] you may spend 1 [[Item Points|Item Point]] to make it [[Healing]] instead of [[Shielding]].

You may consume [[Marked]] applied by any [[Rules/Characters/index|Character]].

When you consume [[Marked]] for [[#Physiology]] on an [[Ally]], you may remove one [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Debuff]] on them.
When you consume [[Marked]] for [[#Physiology]] on an [[Enemy]], the [[Statuses and Conditions/index|Debuff]] applied has a duration of [[Combat]].
