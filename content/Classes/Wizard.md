# Overview
Science and Study will figure out the mysteries of the universe. The wizard bends the laws of magic by stealing spells from every other class, and using their massive repertoire of spells in creative situations. Give a Wizard enough time and they will rewrite reality to make your existence impossible.

| **Action:**         | [[Ask]] what the spell's name is. Collect it for the [[Scenes\|Scene]].<br><br>If you collect three of the same spell in a [[Scenes\|Scene]], for the scene you gain the ability to cast that spell once this scene without using [[Spells and Paradigms/Spells/index\|Mana]].<br>Additionally if the spell is not [[Unique]] or is [[Unique]] to Wizards, you may begin a [[Clock]] to learn that spell school. |

When you complete the [[Clock]]:
- If it is the first spell in the spell school: You learn that spell school and the first spell.
- Otherwise if you know all the lower level spells in that spell school, you learn that spell.
