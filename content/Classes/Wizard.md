# Overview
Science and Study will figure out the mysteries of the universe. The wizard bends the laws of magic by stealing spells from every other class, and using their massive repertoire of spells in creative situations. Give a Wizard enough time and they will kill you before you were born.

# Features

## Study
You gain the following reaction

|                     | Study                                                                                   |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Tempo:**          | 4                                                                                       |
| **Pre-Requisites:** | You have [[Scan\|Scanned]] the target that triggered this reaction this round           |
| **Trigger:**        | A [[Rules/Characters/index\|Character]] in your [[Seek]] performs the [[Magic]] action. |
|                     |                                                                                         |
| **Action:**         | [[Ask]] what the spell's name is. Collect it for the [[Scenes\|Scene]].                 |
| **Tags:**           | [[Rules/Actions/Tags/Reaction\|Reaction]]                                               |

If you collect three of the same spell in a [[Scenes|Scene]], for the scene you gain the ability to cast that spell once this scene.
Additionally if the spell is not [[Unique]] or is [[Unique]] to Wizards, you may begin a [[Clock]] to learn that spell school.

When you complete the [[Clock]] if it is the first spell in the spell school, you learn that spell school and the first spell.
Otherwise if you know all the lower level spells in that spell school, you learn that spell.   

## Quick Study
The [[Tempo]] of [[#Study]] is reduced to 2.
