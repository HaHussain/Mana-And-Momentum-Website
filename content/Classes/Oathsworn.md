The Oathsworn swears an oath, to another, to an organisation, or to themselves. By limiting themselves in some ways, they gain powers in other ways.

# Ideas

Mid way between Paladin and Warlock.

Whats the difference between this and Knight?

Oaths
- Variety of Oaths they can swear, and increasing number of oaths.
- Each oath should give you powers that match that oath's identity.
- If you follow oaths you gain Momentum or Mana? Mana starts at 0 unlike other classes.
- Oaths to an organisation, person or self?
- Oath to an organisation like a Paladin, to Folk like a folk hero, to a person like a knight, to yourself.
- Arthurian

Limited:
 - Punished if you break your oaths in some way?
 - Less powerful if you dont follow your oaths, but don't give a player no options.
