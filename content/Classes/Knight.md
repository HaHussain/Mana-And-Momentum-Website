# Abilities

## Effort
The Knight may [[Exert]] once per [[Scenes|Scene]] without suffering any negative consequences.
When the Knight [[Exert|Exerts]] to retry a [[Skill]] roll they may add +1d6 to the roll. 
When the Knight [[Exert|Exerts]] to take the [[Attack]] action, they may add +1d6 to the [[Hit]] roll of all [[Strike|Strikes]] during that action.

## A
The Knight gains a Weapon Focus 1 with a [[Weapons|Weapon]] their choice.
The Knight gains Armour Focus 1 with an [[Armour]] of their choice.

When the Knight enters combat they may immediately [[Free]]: [[Switch Stance]] at Tempo 0.

The Knight starts with +1 [[Organisations#Reputation|Reputation]] with [[Organisations#Folk|Folk]] and +1 [[Rules/Characters/index#Attitude|Attitude]] with the common working class people.

## B
The Knight may [[Exert]] twice per [[Scenes|Scene]] without suffering any negative consequences.

## Final
The Knight can be in 2 [[Spells and Stances/Stances/index|Stances]] at the same time, but if they are, they must spend a [[Momentum]] at the start of each of their turns.

## Focuses
The Knight gains Weapon Focus 2 with a [[Weapons|Weapon]] of their choice.
The Knight gains Armour Focus 2 with an [[Armour]] of their choice.
